About The Event

A week to co-innovate, lead and sustain

The second edition of the Sustainability Week organized by Gulf University – Bahrain is vocal to promote sustainable practices as adopted by the UN's 17 SDGs. Under the theme "Co-innovating, Leading, Sustaining…", it is designed towards a broader collaborative approach at institutional levels involving Gulf University Bahrain & GCC Partner Institutions.

The event brings together students, faculty, staff, professional bodies, government, industry experts, researchers and individuals representing the community in activities and initiatives that strengthen environmental, social, and economic sustainability.

Across five days, expect discussions, workshops, hands-on activities and competitions aligned with the 17 SDGs — to inspire innovative projects, foster collaborations between students, faculty, industries & NGOs, and support community engagement for sustainable actions.
