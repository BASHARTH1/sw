About The Event

A week to co-innovate, lead and sustain

The Third Edition of Sustainability Week, jointly organized by Gulf University in collaboration with Bahrain Polytechnic, is a strategic academic-industry platform dedicated to advancing sustainability practices aligned with the United Nations 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Building on previous successful editions, the event fosters interdisciplinary collaboration, knowledge exchange, and innovation-driven dialogue among students, academics, industry professionals, and policymakers, reinforcing the role of higher education institutions as catalysts for sustainable development.

This edition places particular emphasis on SDG 9.5: Enhancing Scientific Research, Technological Capabilities and Innovation, highlighting the critical role of research and development in addressing contemporary sustainability challenges.
